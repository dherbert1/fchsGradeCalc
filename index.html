Calculation Information

FCHS uses a 45%-45%-10% grading scale. 45% of your semester grade is made of each quarter, and 10% is from your final exam or project. The calculator above uses some rounding to get the easy to read numbers, so be aware that the actual grade on your report card may be calculated slightly differently.
